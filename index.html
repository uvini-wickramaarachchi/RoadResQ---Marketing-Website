About RoadResQ

Revolutionizing roadside assistance in Sri Lanka

RoadResQ is an innovative mobile-based roadside assistance platform designed to support drivers facing unexpected vehicle breakdowns in Sri Lanka. We connect users with nearby verified garages, mechanics, and towing services in real-time, reducing delays and improving safety during emergencies.

Our platform provides dual interfaces for both users and service providers, enabling features such as live mechanic tracking, automated service assignment, secure payments, and post-service feedback. We aim to streamline the roadside assistance process by offering a centralized, transparent, and efficient service network.
